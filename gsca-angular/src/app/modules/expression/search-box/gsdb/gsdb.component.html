  • Methods

    • Based on normalized and batch corrected RSEM mRNA expression, GSCA provides differential expression analysis for 14 TCGA cancer types (THCA, KIRP, BLCA, LIHC, HNSC, BRCA, LUAD, PRAD, ESCA, KICH, LUSC, KIRC, STAD, COAD), which have over ten paired tumor and normal samples. The fold change is calculated by mean(Tumor) / mean(Normal), the p-value was estimated by t-test and was further adjusted by FDR.

  • Interpretation
    • Table:

      • The table presents detailed information about differential expression analysis, including Cancer type, Gene symbol, Expr. tumor, Expr. normal, Fold change, FDR and # samples. [Expr.: Expression]

    • GSCA provides result figures, including:
      • Bubble plot:

        • The bubble plot presents the fold change and FDR though bubble color and size. The row is the gene set symbol, and column is the selected cancer types. The bubble color from purple to red represents the fold change between tumor vs. normal. The size of dots is positively correlated with the FDR significance.

      • Boxplot:

        • By clicking any one button in the table, GSCA would show a boxplot for the gene expression between tumor and normal samples in single cancer (click cancer type name) or multiple cancer (click gene symbol name).
