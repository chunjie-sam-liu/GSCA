  • Methods

    • The Immune infiltration & CNV module is used to estimate the association between gene CNV (Copy Number Variation) and immune cells’ infiltrates through Spearman correlation analysis. The infiltrates of 24 immune cells were evaluated through ImmuCellAI.

  • Interpretation

    • GSCA provides two forms of results, including table and figure.

    • The result table:

      • GSCA presents detailed information about Cancer type, Gene symbol, Cell type, Correlation and FDR.

      Cell type: the immune cell type, including Dendritic cell, B cell and et al, please find the detailed information of 24 immune cells on document

      Correlation: the Spearman correlation coefficient between specific gene CNV and specific immune cell’s infiltrate.

      FDR: the false discovery rate.

    • GSCA provides result figures including:
      • Bubble plot:

        • By clicking the cancer type button, GSCA will give you a bubble plot presents the summary of the correlation between inputted genes’ CNV and 24 immune cell types infiltrates in selected cancer. Bubble size are correlate with FDR significance. Black outline border indicates FDR < 0.05.

      • Scatter plot:

        • By clicking gene symbol buttons in tables, users can get the detailed result of single gene’s CNV correlated with specific immune cell infiltrate through scatter plot, with fitting line.
