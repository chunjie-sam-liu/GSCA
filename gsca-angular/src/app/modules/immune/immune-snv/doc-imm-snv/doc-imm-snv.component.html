  • Methods

    • The Immune infiltration & SNV module estimates the difference in immune cells’ infiltrates between mutated and WT groups through the Wilcoxon test. The infiltrates of 24 immune cells were evaluated through ImmuCellAI.

  • Interpretation

    • GSCA provides two forms of results, including tables and figures.

    • Table:

      • The table presents detailed information about Cancer type, Gene symbol, Cell type, Log2(FC), and FDR.

      Cell type: the immune cell type, including Dendritic cell, B cell, and et al., please find the detailed information of 24 immune cell types in the document

      Log2(FC): Log2 fold change of the abundance of immune cells in the Mutated group compared with that in the WT group.

      FDR: the false discovery rate.

    • GSCA provides result figures, including:
      • Bubble plot:

        • By clicking the cancer type button, GSCA will give you a bubble plot that presents the summary of the association between inputted genes' SNV and 24 immune cell types' infiltrates in selected cancers. Bubble size correlates with FDR significance. The black outline border indicates FDR ≤ 0.05.

      • Box plot:

        • By clicking gene symbol buttons in the tables, users can get the difference of a specific immune cell's infiltrate level between groups through a box plot.
