  • Methods

    • The Immune infiltration & SNV module estimates the difference of immune cells’ infiltrates between mutated and WT groups through Wilcoxon test. The infiltrates of 24 immune cells were evaluated through ImmuCellAI.

  • Interpretation

    • GSCA provides two forms of results, including table and figure.

    • The result table:

      • Presents detailed information about Cancer type, Gene symbol, Cell type, Log2(FC) and FDR.

      Cell type: the immune cell type, including Dendritic cell, B cell and et al, please find the detailed information of 24 immune cells on document

      Log2(FC): Log2 fold change of specific immune cell’s infiltratethe in Mutated group compared with that in WT group.

      FDR: the false discovery rate.

    • GSCA provides result figures including:
      • Bubble plot:

        • By clicking the cancer type button, GSCA will give you a bubble plot presents the summary of the association between inputted genes’ SNV and 24 immune cell types infiltrates in selected cancer. Bubble size are correlate with FDR significance. Black outline border indicates FDR < 0.05.

      • Box plot:

        • By clicking gene symbol buttons in tables, users can get the detailed result of specific immune cell infiltrate level between groups through box plot.
