  • Methods

    • Illumina HumanMethylation 450k level 3 data were downloaded from TCGA database. In total, 14 cancer types (THCA, KIRP, BLCA, LIHC, HNSC, BRCA, LUAD, PRAD, ESCA, KICH, LUSC, KIRC, STAD, COAD), which have more than 10 paired tumor and adjacent non-tumor samples, were selected and processed into this analysis.

    Generally, there are multiple methylation sites in the region of one gene. Therefore, there are multiple tags store the methylation level of each site. Before differential methylation analysis, we performed correlation analysis to filter the site most negatively correlate with gene expression.

    The fold change is calculated by mean(Tumor) / mean(Normal), p-value was estimated by t-test and was further adjusted by FDR.
