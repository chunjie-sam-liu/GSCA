  • Methods

    • We collected the IC50 of 481 small molecules in 1001 cell lines, and its corresponding mRNA gene expression from Genomics of Therapeutics Response Portal (CTRP). The mRNA expression data and drug sensitivity data were merged. Pearson correlation analysis was performed to get the correlation between gene mRNA expression and drug IC50. P-value was adjusted by FDR.
